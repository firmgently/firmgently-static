Your Privacy on this site

Tracking

This site does not track or analyse you.

Access Logging

This site is served via Netlify. They keep access logs of visitors including IP addresses. Netlify retain those logs for less than 30 days and are GDPR compliant.

Contact Form

The contact form is processed by Netlify. Information entered into the form will be stored by Netlify so that I can read and reply to it. They will log your IP address but the information will not be used for tracking or shared with anybody else.

Cookies

This site stores a cookie on your computer to remember if you want to view the light or dark styling. The cookie is only used for this purpose and is not shared with anybody else.
